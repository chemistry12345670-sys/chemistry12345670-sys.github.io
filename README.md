<!-- Lahore -->
<div class="city" id="lahore">
<h2>Lahore</h2>
<div class="gallery">
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/6b/Badshahi_Mosque_Lahore.jpg">
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/4/44/Lahore_Fort_view.jpg">
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/6f/Minar-e-Pakistan_Lahore.jpg">
</div>
<p><b>Introduction:</b> Lahore is the cultural and historical heart of Pakistan. It has served as a capital for several empires including the Mughal Empire, leaving behind magnificent architecture, gardens, forts and mosques.</p>
<p><b>Culture:</b> Lahore represents Punjabi culture with music, poetry, festivals, traditional clothing and strong literary traditions.</p>
<p><b>Famous Food:</b> Nihari, Paye, Lahori Chargha, Halwa Puri.</p>
<p><b>Famous Places:</b> Badshahi Mosque, Lahore Fort, Minar-e-Pakistan.</p>
